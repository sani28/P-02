Green Tea 綠茶 Green Teas can be divided into four categories, Grassy, Dark, Wild and Organic Green. Grassy Green Tea is grassy in taste, different grassy green tea provides different aftertastes and aromas, high in antioxidant. If you are looking for Green Tea with high contents of antioxidants that rejuvenates and boost your immune system that would be a good choice. Dark Green Tea undergoes a different drying method such as sun-dried or rolling heat dried. The teas are darker and stronger in taste typically can be consumed as a natural cleansing agent. Wild Green Tea is air dried and high in antioxidant. Our wild green tea, Bittermint is particularly effective for liver cleansing, ask as how!

Organic Green Tea is certified by IMO Switzerland, selection available in both Grassy and Dark Green. Organic Teas growing uses no synthetic fertilizers or pesticides. It is a form of agriculture that relies on methods like compost and biological pest control to maintain soil productivity and control pest on a farm. Good choice at all time!!

Jasmine Tea 茉莉

Jasmine teas are blended with green tea, the quality of green tea depends on the grade. The higher the grade the higher the quality of green tea and numbers of time it goes thru the blended of jasmine tips for aroma.

For example the Yin Hao Jasmine Tea (First Grade) is blended with high quality green tea and jasmine silver tips over 21 times. The more silver tips it has the more smoothness it offers. Dragon Ball Jasmine is the top of the line. It is picked from the bud of jasmine trees, super aromatic, smooth, and can last for many infusions. Relax with the scent and taste!
